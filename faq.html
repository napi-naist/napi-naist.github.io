Who can apply for the NAPI Program?

Bona fide students from any course under the Ateneo de Manila University (ADMU) School of Science and Engineering (SOSE) may apply. They must be:

  • Currently enrolled as an undergraduate student in their final two years;
  • Currently enrolled as a student in a master's degree program.

What if multiple applicants are interested in the same laboratory?

Successful applicants will be assigned to one of their chosen laboratories. If the laboratory you selected is not able to accommodate you, you will be assigned to the one nearest to your field of interest. It is recommended that you choose the laboratories that match your current research interests or can provide the technical skills you wish to acquire.


What costs are covered?

The round trip plane fare (Manila to Osaka and back), transportation to and from the airport, and accommodations during the internship period are covered.


What do I do once accepted?

You may send an email to your host professor to introduce yourself and discuss the topics you will be working on during your internship.
