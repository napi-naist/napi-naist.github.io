Application Procedure

Follow the steps below to formally apply to the NAPI program

  1. Check if you are eligible to apply.
  2. Attend the NAPI Information Session at ADMU on {{ info.information_session_admu }} (optional but recommended)
  3. Decide on a first and second choice laboratory here.
  4. Prepare these documents ahead of time:
    • Scanned copy of passport
    • Scanned copy of Certificate of Enrollment or Graduation (This document is available at the Office of the Registrar. *Note that the Certificate of Enrollment is different from the tuition receipt.)
  5. Please ask permission from your Thesis Adviser, Department Chair, Program Director, or other ADMU faculty member to complete the Recommendation Form for your NAPI application. The Recommendation Form will be sent to your recommender's email after you submit the Application Form below.
  • Finish the Application Form by {{ info.application_deadline }}
  • Remind your recommender to complete the Recommendation Form by {{ info.recommender_deadline }}


    • Handling of Personal Information

    The collected documents, including the application form, passport copy, and certificate of enrollment or graduation, will be used solely for the purposes of screening and processing applications for this program. All personal information will be handled with strict confidentiality and in accordance with relevant data protection regulations. Documents will not be shared with any third parties unrelated to the selection process and will be securely disposed of after use.
